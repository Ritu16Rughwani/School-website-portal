Admissions:

Admissions in Sainik School Satara are meant for boys only. The entrance examination is held for admission to class Vl & lX. Boys who are between the age of 10-11 years and 13-1-4 years respectively on 1st July of the year in which admission is sought are eligible to take the examination.
